Mission Report

◆ Architecture Decisions & Trade-offs

Decision 1: JWT (stateless) over Session (stateful)

Rationale: Stateless design, zero-cost horizontal scaling, no Redis required.

Trade-off: Tokens cannot be immediately revoked after issuance.

⚠ Note: If forced-logout is needed later, implement token blacklist.

Decision 2: bcrypt (cost=12) over argon2id

Rationale: Mature ecosystem, no native compilation dependency.

Trade-off: ~40% slower than argon2 at equivalent security level.

📝 argon2id considered but node-argon2 has build issues on Alpine Linux.
